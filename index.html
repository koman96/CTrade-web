Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2025

Thank you for using CTrade. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("CTrade", "we", "our", or "us").

1. Local Application Notice

CTrade is a local utility app. It operates entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection for core features. We do not collect or transmit your personal data to our servers.

2. Information We May Access

While CTrade does not actively collect your personal data, the app may access certain device functions to perform as intended, including:

  • Storage Access: To save timer history or user preferences on your device.
  • Notifications: To remind you when your tea brewing timer completes.

All such data remains stored locally on your device and is not shared with us or third parties.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identity information (such as name, email, or contact details)
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Third-party tracking data

4. Optional User Communication

If you choose to contact our support team, we may receive information such as your email address and your message content. This information is used solely to assist you and improve your experience. We do not share this information with third parties.

5. Data Security

Since all personal data remains stored locally on your device, you are responsible for protecting access to your device. We recommend using device-level security such as PIN codes, passwords, or biometric locks to safeguard your information.

6. Children's Privacy

CTrade is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware of any such data being provided to us, we will promptly delete it.
